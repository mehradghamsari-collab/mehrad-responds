Privacy Policy — mehrad.responds

Last updated: 3 July 2026

mehrad.responds is a personal, single-operator tool used exclusively by the owner of the Instagram account @mehrad.knows to automatically respond to direct messages and comments received by that account. It is not offered as a service to any third party and has no other users.

What data the app processes

  • The text of direct messages and comments sent to @mehrad.knows via Instagram
  • The Instagram-scoped sender ID and, for comments, the public username of the sender
  • A log of the automated replies the app sends

How the data is used

Incoming message and comment text is used solely to decide on and generate an automated reply. When keyword rules do not apply, the message text may be processed by Google's Gemini API to compose a reply. Data is never used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than replying to the message it belongs to.

Storage and retention

Activity logs and short conversation context are stored in a private, access-controlled Supabase database owned by the operator, and are periodically deleted. Data is never sold or shared with third parties beyond the processors named above (Meta Platforms, Google, Supabase) as strictly required to operate the service.

Data deletion

To request deletion of any data related to your Instagram interactions with @mehrad.knows, send a direct message to @mehrad.knows on Instagram or email rezaghamighami@gmail.com with the subject "Data deletion request". All stored data associated with your account will be removed within 30 days.
